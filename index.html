About Me

Hossein Mahvash Mohammadi received the B.S.and M.S. degree in Computer Engineering from the Amir Kabir University, Tehran, Iran in 1992 and 1997 respectively. He started the PhD in Computer Engineering at Sharif university of technology in 2002. He was visiting researcher at Center on Intelligent Machines in McGill university in 2004. Then he exchanged his PhD study to Electrical Engineering at Ecole Polytechnique of Montreal, where he received his PhD in 2009.
